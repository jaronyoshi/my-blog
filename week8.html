01

This week, I tried my best to break down the strucuture oof my presentation and really focus on the tone of the presentation and the message I wanted to convey. In my last presentation I was told that my presentation wasn't really pitching and rather like a student so that's where my focus was. I had just gone to the start up weekend event so I learnt a lot about how to pitch and what sort of content I should have in my pitch presentation and I feel that I executed that pretty well.

02

Looking back at the whole term rather than just this week, I feel that my biggest weakness is branding and designing the user interface. I feel I failed to design the app and brand for the purpose and the problem it is solving. I took a long time to even come up with the design I eventually decided going with and I am not even slightly happy with it. I still don't know how to get better at it besides just practicing and repeating over and over again.

03

Again looking back at the whole term, I noted down which areas I need to focus on for next term. For researchm I need to make sure I ask the right questions and make sure I ask the 5 why's. I also need to try and have a even better understanding of my audience before I go into design next time. As mentioned before I feel I am terrible at brandong and UI so I need to work on that. Another aprt I found difficult was layout in the UI. I also need to learn how components and variants work in figma.
